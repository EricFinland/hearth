# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be respectful. hearth is a place to build interesting things together, not to
tear people down.

## Expected behavior

- Be welcoming and patient with people of all backgrounds and experience levels.
- Critique ideas and code, not people. Assume good faith.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully, and give it kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language of any kind.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces, GitHub issues, pull requests, and
discussions, and to anyone representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior through the contact form at
https://ericcatalano.dev/#contact. Reports will be reviewed and handled
confidentially. The maintainer may remove comments, commits, or contributors
that violate this code.

This is a lightweight adaptation of the spirit of the Contributor Covenant.
